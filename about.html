About Harvest Hands Foundation

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Johannesburg, South Africa, Harvest Hands Foundation emerged from a simple observation: while food scarcity affected many communities, the potential for sustainable agriculture remained largely untapped.

What began as a small initiative to establish community gardens has grown into a comprehensive program addressing food poverty through multiple approaches.

Over the past eight years, we've established 25 community gardens across Johannesburg's low-income areas and conducted weekly nutrition education seminars in partnership with local schools and community centers.

Our Mission

"To empower communities through sustainable agriculture and equitable access to nutritious food."

Our Vision

"A South Africa where every household has access to fresh, healthy produce."
