Get Involved With Our Mission

Join us in creating a South Africa where every household has access to fresh, healthy produce. There are several ways you can contribute to our mission.

Volunteer

We need dedicated volunteers to help with:

  • Community garden maintenance
  • Food distribution
  • Nutrition education workshops
  • Administrative support
  • Event organization

No prior experience is necessary - we provide all the training you need!

Volunteer Now

Corporate Partnerships

Businesses can support our mission through:

  • Financial sponsorship
  • Employee volunteer programs
  • In-kind donations (tools, seeds, equipment)
  • CSR initiatives
  • Event sponsorship

Partner with us to make a meaningful impact in South African communities.

Partner With Us

Community Groups

Schools, churches, and community organizations can:

  • Host a nutrition workshop
  • Start a community garden at your location
  • Organize a food drive
  • Arrange a volunteer day for your members
  • Help identify families in need of support
